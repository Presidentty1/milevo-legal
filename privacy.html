Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 28, 2026

Introduction

Milevo ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Business Profile: Business name, email, phone, address, logo (stored locally on your device)
  • Client Information: Client names, contact details, notes
  • Invoice Data: Invoice details, line items, payment records, milestones
  • Project Data: Project names, budgets, time entries

Local-First Architecture

All your business data is stored exclusively on your device. Milevo uses Apple's SwiftData framework for local storage. We do not operate servers, databases, or cloud storage. Your invoices, clients, and projects never leave your iPhone unless you explicitly export or share them.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Anonymous Usage Analytics: We collect anonymous, aggregated usage data (e.g., which features are used, screen views, session counts) through PostHog analytics to improve the app experience.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version (collected anonymously with analytics events)

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Location Data: We do not track your location
  • Advertising Trackers: No ad SDKs, no data sold to advertisers
  • Account Information: No login, no email collection, no accounts
  • Financial Data: We do not process payments or access bank accounts. All billing is handled by Apple via In-App Purchase.

How We Use Your Information

Core Functionality

  • Create and manage invoices, estimates, clients, and projects
  • Track time entries and milestones
  • Generate PDF invoices for export and sharing

Analytics (Opt-Out Available)

  • Understand which features are most valuable to users
  • Identify and fix bugs
  • Improve app performance and user experience

You can disable analytics at any time in Settings → Privacy → Analytics. When disabled, no usage data is collected.

Data Storage & Security

  • Storage: 100% local on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • Encryption: Your device's built-in encryption protects all stored data
  • Backups: If you use iCloud Backup, your Milevo data is included in your encrypted device backup
  • No Server: We do not operate any servers or remote databases

Third-Party Services

  • PostHog: Anonymous usage analytics (opt-out available in Settings)
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management — processes only anonymous purchase receipts, no personal data
  • Apple In-App Purchase: All payment processing is handled by Apple

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal data to any third party.

Your Rights & Choices

Data Control

  • View: All your data is visible within the app at all times
  • Export: Export invoices as PDF from the app
  • Delete: Delete individual invoices, clients, or projects at any time. Use Settings → Data → Erase All Data to permanently delete everything.
  • Analytics Opt-Out: Settings → Privacy → Analytics toggle

GDPR (European Users)

  • Legal Basis: Consent and legitimate interest
  • Rights: Access, rectification, erasure, portability
  • Contact: milevoapp@gmail.com

CCPA (California Users)

  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell personal information
  • Rights: Access, deletion, opt-out
  • Contact: milevoapp@gmail.com

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
