Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first invoice?

Tap the + button on the Dashboard or Invoices tab. Fill in your client details, add line items, and tap Save. You can then preview, share, or export the invoice as a PDF.

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not use cloud servers or remote databases. Make sure to keep regular device backups via iCloud or Finder.

How many free invoices can I create?

Milevo's free tier allows you to create and send up to 3 invoices. After that, you'll need Milevo Pro (Monthly, Annual, or Lifetime) to continue creating invoices.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your iPhone's Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions → Milevo → Cancel Subscription. Your Pro access will continue until the end of the current billing period.

How do I restore my purchases on a new device?

Open Milevo on your new device, go to the paywall (Settings → Upgrade), and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID.

How do I delete all my data?

Go to Settings → Data → Erase All Data. This will permanently delete all invoices, clients, projects, and business data from your device. This cannot be undone.

Can I export my invoices?

Yes! Open any invoice, tap Preview, then use the Share button to export as PDF. You can send it via email, save to Files, AirDrop, or use any other sharing method.

Is Milevo available on iPad or Mac?

Milevo is currently available for iPhone only. iPad and Mac support may be added in a future update.
